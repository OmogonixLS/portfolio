Skills & Experience

- 2+ years experience using Godot.

- I make Godot tutorials sometimes on YouTube (Nearly 14k subscribers).

- I believe I have a good understanding of GDScript and other aspects of Godot.

- 18 Godot games released on Itchio (mostly small ones, one big project is in the works and has a Steam page).

- A number of years previous experience with Unity and C# (+2 Steam game releases).

- Always improving my art skills: I love modelling in Blender, making textures, logos, etc.

- Understanding of Blender and experience in aspects like rigging, cloth physics, and UV mapping.

- Most of my games are currently themed around horror.

- I love challenging and improving myself.
